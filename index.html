About This Book

The perfect First Communion gift — a stunning visual keepsake your child will treasure for a lifetime.

Most children arrive at the altar dressed beautifully — but confused. They memorize prayers without knowing what they mean. They receive Jesus for the first time without understanding the miracle taking place in their hands.

This book changes that.

My First Holy Communion through Infographics combines early reader text with 20+ detailed infographics — making the faith come alive for children ages 6–9 in a way no ordinary book can.

What's Inside

  • The story of Jesus — His life, the Last Supper, the Resurrection — beautifully illustrated
  • Step-by-step guide to Confession — so your child arrives prepared and confident
  • Exactly what happens at Mass — a complete visual walkthrough of every moment
  • How to receive Holy Communion — both options explained clearly with illustrations
  • Sacred symbols explained — the chalice, the host, the candle, the rosary, the white garment
  • The Our Father — every line visualized in stunning Renaissance-inspired illustration
  • 2,000 years of the Eucharist — a historical journey from the Last Supper to today
  • How to Pray the Rosary — complete step-by-step guide
  • Interactive memory pages — for the child to fill in and treasure forever
