Privacy Policy for Prayer Trainer

Effective date: May 23, 2026

Prayer Trainer is a Catholic prayer learning app designed to help users read, listen to, practice, and memorize prayers. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information.

Developer: Discover Visually
Privacy contact: discovervisually@gmail.com
App package: com.prayertrainer.app

Summary

Prayer Trainer is designed to be local-first.

  • No account is required.
  • Prayer progress stays on your device.
  • The app does not request microphone, camera, contacts, or location access.
  • Prayer reminders are optional.
  • The current app uses bundled prayer text, images, and audio.
  • Some optional resources or premium packs may be offered for purchase in the future.

Information We Collect

Prayer Trainer does not collect personal information on our servers in the current version.

The app stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Prayer progress, including practiced and memorized prayers
  • XP, level progress, and saint card unlock progress
  • Reminder settings, including whether reminders are enabled and selected reminder times
  • First-run setup and dismissed-screen preferences
  • Premium unlock state, when premium purchase support is enabled

This information is stored locally on your device using Android local storage. It is not sent to the developer in the current version.

Permissions

Prayer Trainer may request the following Android permission:

  • Notifications: used only for optional morning and evening prayer reminders.

The notification permission is not requested on first launch. It is requested only after you choose to enable prayer reminders.

Prayer Trainer does not request:

  • Microphone access
  • Camera access
  • Location access
  • Contacts access

Prayer Reminders

Prayer reminders are optional. If you enable reminders, the app schedules local Android notifications for the selected morning and evening times.

You can disable reminders in the app or in your device system settings.

Audio

The current version uses bundled audio files for supported prayers. Audio plays locally on your device.

Future versions may allow optional audio downloads. If added, those downloads should happen only when you choose them.

Purchases and Parent Resources

Some optional prayer packs, parent resources, or related products may be offered for purchase. Any future in-app purchases will be handled through Google Play Billing.

Prayer Trainer may also show optional parent resources, such as a First Communion book recommendation. Opening an external store link may take you outside the app, where the external service's own privacy policy applies.

Ads

The current release build does not include an active ads SDK.

If ads are added in a future version, this Privacy Policy and the Google Play Data Safety form should be updated before release to describe any ad SDK data collection or sharing.

Calm, Anxiety, and Spiritual Support

Prayers in calm, worry, or anxiety-related categories are for spiritual support only. They are not medical, psychological, or mental health advice. If you or someone else needs medical or mental health support, please contact a qualified professional or local emergency services.

Children and Families

Prayer Trainer is intended for general Catholic prayer learning, including adults and families. Children should use the app with a parent or guardian where appropriate.

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Sharing

In the current version, Prayer Trainer does not share personal user data with the developer or third parties.

If future versions add Google Play Billing, ads, analytics, crash reporting, cloud sync, or downloadable content, the relevant third-party services and data handling practices must be disclosed here before release.

Data Retention and Deletion

Local progress data remains on your device until you delete it.

You can delete local app data by:

  • Uninstalling Prayer Trainer
  • Clearing the app's storage from Android system settings

Because the current version does not create accounts or store personal data on developer servers, there is no server-side account deletion process.

Security

Prayer Trainer keeps current progress data locally on your device. The app does not transmit this data to developer servers in the current version.

Your device operating system is responsible for protecting app-local storage according to Android platform security controls.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when the app changes, especially if future versions add purchases, ads, analytics, downloads, accounts, or cloud sync.

The updated version should be posted at the public Privacy Policy URL used in Google Play Console.
